Keynote

Mine the IoT Skills Gap
Architecting User-Centric Competitive Product Advantage

Sarah Cooper, COO at M2Mi

50% of US households have Wi-Fi.
64% of adults have smartphones.
Privacy - if you aren't paying for the service, your data is the product.
60% like the wearable device, but only 20% like the associated app.
Expectations for the app are very high.
Device is single point of truth.
Every device is part of a process.
Synchronize a process without scripting the process.
Users have context too - will device still be intuitive in 15 years?
Ecosystems apply pressure on interoperability and shared control.
Code for the future.
